How to Buy $CLIVE

Ready to join Clive and Phin on their journey to the moon? Follow these simple steps to get your $CLIVE tokens!

1

Setup Your Wallet

  • Download and install a compatible Abstract wallet (Abstract Global, etc.)
  • Create a new wallet or import your existing one
  • Make sure to securely back up your seed phrase
  • Configure your wallet settings for optimal security
2

Fund Your Wallet

  • Transfer ETH to your wallet from an exchange or another wallet
  • Ensure you have enough ETH for the purchase and gas fees
  • Double-check all transaction details before confirming
  • Wait for the transaction to complete (usually takes a few minutes)
3

Bridge to Abstract ETH

  • Navigate to the Abstract Bridge in your wallet
  • Select ETH as your source token and Abstract ETH as the destination
  • Enter the amount you wish to bridge
  • Confirm the transaction and wait for it to complete
  • Verify that the Abstract ETH appears in your wallet
4

Swap for $CLIVE

  • Open the Swap interface in your wallet
  • Choose Abstract ETH as your input token
  • Search for and select $CLIVE as your output token
  • Enter the amount you wish to swap
  • Review the transaction details, including fees
  • Confirm the swap and wait for it to process
  • Congratulations! You now own $CLIVE tokens
